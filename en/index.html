Simple & fast

You can specify exactly when you want to lose how much and the app will calculate how many calories you can consume based on this information. This gives you a clear goal and you don't have to fight your way through a seemingly endless diet.

Fit & Healthy

Osis.Fit is not just a calorie counter app, so you can also take advantage of many features if you don't want to lose weight. Among other things, you can also save the exercises and training plans for your fitness center visit very conveniently in the app and therefore always have them with you. Or discover new plans and activities to constantly improve your physical health.

Professional

We are very precise in developing and checking our formulas and also work together with Swiss recognised doctors. The methods are basically the same as for many other fitness and weight loss applications (WeightWatchers, BodyChange etc.) and have often proven their effectiveness in the past. The most important factor for the correctness of the calculations and thus your success is always you - therefore it is important that you keep your data (weight, consumed calories and additional activities) constantly up to date.
